## Recording protocol

- Safety take FIRST, right after prototype freeze (single shot, <2:00, exported and played back).
- Record 1080p; use a clean desktop, hide notifications and close personal tabs.
- Read the bold spoken text verbatim. Use the same sentences as SRT captions,
  split at natural punctuation and never paraphrase a metric.
- Keep the pointer still except for the incident, Service and approval clicks.
- Export MP4 + SRT; verify duration, legibility and audio, then play both final
  local files from beginning to end.
